About lexpress-petites-annonces.mu Launched in 2009, www.lexpressclassifieds.mu has been rebranded in 2019 and has now become www.lexpress-petites-annonces.mu. Every year, the website connects more than 500 000 people with businesses and individuals around the island. Lexpress-petites-annonces.mu hosts classified ads in 18 categories ranging from properties to services.
